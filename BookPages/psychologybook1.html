Book Excerpt

In this smart, practical book, Kelly McGonigal shows that stress isn’t nearly as bad as its reputation. In fact, if we change our mindsets just a bit, we can transform stress from a barrier that thwarts to a resource that propels us. The Upside of Stress is a perfect how-to guide for anyone who wants to tap into the biology of courage and the psychology of thriving under pressure.” —Daniel H. Pink, author of Drive and To Sell Is Human

“A fascinating tour of cutting-edge research on how stress affects us in ways, both good and bad, that we never suspect.
